ENDING: GOLD FEVER

Deep in the mine, you strike a vein of gold. You become the wealthiest man in the territory, buying up the entire town. But wealth can't buy back the thrill of the gunfight. You often stand on your porch, staring at the sunset, wondering what could have been.

Your name becomes synonymous with justice in the frontier. Years later, a young boy asks you about the day you faced Blackwood. You just smile, tip your hat, and ride into the sunset. The legend never dies.
