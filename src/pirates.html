You awaken on a tropical beach, the wreckage of your ship scattered along the sand. Clutched in your hand is a torn treasure map pointing to the heart of Isla Calavera. A rusty cutlass lies half-buried nearby. Inland, dense jungle obscures the island's interior. To the north, you spot the mast of a beached shipwreck.

Armed with the cutlass, you follow the map into the jungle. The trail splits. To the left, ancient stone steps climb toward a crumbling temple. To the right, you hear the sounds of a pirate camp and drunken shanties.

Unarmed, you stumble upon the beached shipwreck before reaching the treeline. The hull is cracked open. Inside, you spot a grappling hook dangling from a broken mast and a chest that might contain supplies. The tide is rising fast.

The temple is overgrown with vines. At its entrance, a stone altar holds a golden idol, but the floor is covered in pressure plates. You can either enter carefully or climb the outer wall to bypass the traps.

You creep close to the camp. A gang of pirates guards a captive navigator and a chest of stolen goods. The pirate captain, a hulking brute with a mechanical hand, barks orders. You can fight them with the cutlass or try to sneak past and free the navigator.

You climb into the dark, creaking wreck. A skeleton still clutching a flintlock pistol grins at you from the corner. You can take the pistol (it might still work) or secure the grappling hook hanging above.

You avoid the pressure plates and reach the inner chamber. A massive stone golem awakens, demanding a treasure offering. You have no gold — but the cutlass might work as a trade. Or you can try to fight the golem.

You scale the temple wall and enter through a shattered window. Inside is a treasure trove, but the idol on the pedestal radiates dark energy. You can grab the treasure and run or examine the idol more closely.

You clash with the pirate captain. Steel rings out. You disarm him, but he triggers the ship's cannons aimed at the island. You can finish him or spare him and take his ship.

With the grappling hook, you climb to the temple's upper level and bypass all traps. You find the treasure room filled with gold and jewels, but also a cursed idol. You can take the gold or touch the idol.

Swimming around, you spot a hidden cave beneath the wreck. Inside rests a skeleton clutching a worn journal. It tells of a sea monster guarding the greatest treasure. You can sail toward the monster's lair or return to shore.

ENDING: ETERNAL CASTAWAY

You return to the beach and build a shelter. Days turn into years. You survive, but the treasure remains a dream. Sometimes, when the tide is low, you still see the map's X marked on the sand.
